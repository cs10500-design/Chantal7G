Information about Art!

One of my favourite forms of Art is,Papier Mâché,because it provides a satisfying
creative outlet that engages multiple senses and skills. Paper mache is a craft technique that combines paper
and a binding agent like glue or paste to create strong, lightweight objects. It can be made by mixing shredded paper
into a pulp or by layering strips of paper soaked in paste over a form, which hardens once it dries.
The term comes from the French for "chewed paper" and is used to make items such as masks, bowls, and sculptures.
